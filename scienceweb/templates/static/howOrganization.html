Organizations

As part of the Society for the Improvement of Science, SACSIS, we are committed to the scientific community. We know the challenges that researches are facing worldwide to raise funding for researchers and/or projects, and how specific vulnerable areas have suffered more than others, specifically due to the scarcity of public funding.

We are a nonprofit organization with the aim of generating jobs (or extra income) for the scientific community and providing funds to scientific, medical, social and technological projects.


Which organizations could benefit from our programs?

You can create your own site at Peerland if you have a laboratory, university department, or specific collaborative project for which you need to raise some funds.

You can have access to our special fundraiser program through Peerland if you are a scientific, medical, technological or other type of society, foundation or nonprofit organization with a specific link to the scientific publishing and peer review systems.


How it works

  • Whether you have a specific project in your laboratory, a technician, a PhD, a postdoc in need of funding, whether you are an environmental organization, a social endeavour project, a scientific organization, societies for the care of specific patient populations, rare diseases, etc., Peerland is your place to explain your specific need or project to the scientific community and raise funds through your peers and your favourite journals.
  • Register your organization, add your project for which you would like to raise funds, set up your goals and keep track of the funds you raise over time.
  • Direct your collaborator peers to work through Peerland when they are asked to perform a peer review or evaluation of a project and receive donations from their charitable work.
  • Get editors to collaborate on your projects through Peerland. We offer to the journals the option of back contribution to scientific research through the peer review work of specialists and the option of contributing to social endeavours by funding projects which ultimately helps in advancing research, and thus generating more publications.
  • Use our blog platform to spread your needs to the scientific community, and get public awareness through our news releases.
